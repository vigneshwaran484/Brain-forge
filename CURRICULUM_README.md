# 🧠 Brain Forge — Curriculum Overview

> **Proficiency-Based Learning | Ages 5–14 | 12 Levels · 6 Pillars · 4 Tiers**

---

## 📐 Six Pillars of Learning

| Pillar | Focus Areas |
|--------|------------|
| 🧮 **Foundational Mathematics** | Number sense, geometry, algebra, statistics, logic, mental math |
| 🔬 **Science** | Life sciences, physics, chemistry, earth science, experiments |
| 📖 **Communicative English** | Grammar, creative writing, comprehension, public speaking, debate |
| 💻 **Cyber Knowledge** | Block coding, programming, AI basics, cyber safety, web development |
| 🌍 **General Knowledge** | History, geography, current affairs, culture, sports, environment |
| 🎨 **Art, Craft & Personality** | Drawing, painting, craft, leadership, emotional intelligence, teamwork |

---

## 🗺️ The 12-Level Mastery Pathway

### Tier 1 — Junior 🌱 (Levels 1–3)

| Level | Title | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 01 | **Junior** | Foundation Building — Number sense basics, phonics, simple experiments, digital awareness, world awareness, basic art skills |
| 02 | **Junior Pro** | Deeper Exploration — Pattern recognition, reading fluency, plant & animal sciences, coding intro, India GK, craft projects |
| 03 | **Junior Master** | Advanced Foundations — Word problems, creative writing, earth sciences, cyber safety, current events, leadership basics |

> 🎯 *Gateway Exam required to advance to Senior Tier*

---

### Tier 2 — Senior 🌳 (Levels 4–6)

| Level | Title | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 04 | **Senior** | Knowledge Expansion — Fractions & decimals, essay writing, human body, programming basics, world geography, emotional intelligence |
| 05 | **Senior Pro** | Complex Connections — Pre-algebra, debate skills, force & energy, algorithms, history deep-dives, team leadership |
| 06 | **Senior Master** | Independent Thinking — Statistics, comprehension mastery, chemical reactions, AI basics, current affairs, public speaking |

> 🏆 *Gateway Exam required to advance to Competition Tier*

---

### Tier 3 — Competition Competent 🏆 (Levels 7–9)

| Level | Title | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 07 | **CC Junior** | Olympiad Basics — Competition math strategies, science olympiad prep, IEO preparation, NCO basics, quiz training |
| 08 | **CC Intermediate** | National Readiness — National-level problem sets, NSO/NSTSE prep, spelling bee, hackathon prep, advanced quiz mastery |
| 09 | **CC Senior** | International Standards — IMO/ISMO level problems, international science, global English proficiency, AI competitions |

> 👑 *Gateway Exam required to advance to Champion Tier*

---

### Tier 4 — Champion 👑 (Levels 10–12)

| Level | Title | Focus |
|-------|-------|-------|
| 10 | **Champion Junior** | Mentorship & Mastery — Peer mentoring, cross-domain projects, innovation challenges, research basics, portfolio building |
| 11 | **Champion Intermediate** | Innovation & Leadership — Original problem creation, teaching practice, community projects, advanced research |
| 12 | **Champion Senior** | Grand Master — Personal excellence portfolio, international competition strategy, legacy project, Grand Master title |

---

## 🏅 Competition Preparation

Brain Forge prepares students for national and international competitions across all 6 pillars:

| Pillar | Competitions |
|--------|-------------|
| Mathematics | IMO, ISMO, Math Kangaroo, SASMO |
| Science | NSO, NSTSE, Science Olympiad |
| English | IEO, Spelling Bee, Essay Competitions |
| Cyber | NCO, Hackathons, AI Challenge |
| General Knowledge | IGKO, Quiz Champs, Current Affairs |
| Art & Personality | IADO, Art Olympiad, Craft Challenge |

## 📊 Assessment Framework

| Type | Description |
|------|------------|
| Formative | Ongoing daily learning progress tracking |
| Module | Topic-level mastery verification |
| Level | Readiness evaluation for advancement |
| HOTS | Critical thinking & higher-order growth |
| Portfolio | Holistic development documentation |
| Gateway Exam | Tier advancement evaluations |

**Minimum 75% score required before advancing to the next level.**
